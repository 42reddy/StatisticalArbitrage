                    │

                    │     ├── Suggest parameters
                    │     ├── Evaluate on all folds (with pruning)
                    │     └── Update surrogate model
                    │
                    ├── Collect best trial + top‑5 trials (score > 0)
                    │
                    └── Store candidates (params, CV score, seed)
            │
            ↓
    All candidates (e.g., 4*(1+5)=24)
            │
            ↓
    Evaluate each candidate on holdout (once, no re‑training)
            │
            ↓
    Select candidate with highest holdout score
            │
            ↓
    Update global PARAMS with those values
